## IPv4

- 匹配每个值

```reg
^(\d|[1-9]\d|1\d\d|2(?:[0-4]\d|5[0-5]))\.(\d|[1-9]\d|1\d\d|2(?:[0-4]\d|5[0-5]))\.(\d|[1-9]\d|1\d\d|2(?:[0-4]\d|5[0-5]))\.(\d|[1-9]\d|1\d\d|2(?:[0-4]\d|5[0-5]))$
```

- 只做验证判断

```reg
^(?:(?:\d|[1-9]\d|1\d\d|2(?:[0-4]\d|5[0-5]))\.){3}(?:\d|[1-9]\d|1\d\d|2(?:[0-4]\d|5[0-5]))$
```
